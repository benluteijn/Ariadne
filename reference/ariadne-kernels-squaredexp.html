SquaredExp

Isotropic squared exponential covariance function also known as the Gaussian kernel or RBF kernel

k(x,y) = σ² exp(-1/(2lengthscale²) * (x - y)²) + δ[x=y] σ²_{noise}

σ² is the signal variance σ²_{noise} is the noise variance
